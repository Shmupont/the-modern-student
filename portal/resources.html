Power Phrases

"Act as a..." Set the AI's perspective (tutor, editor, study partner)
"Explain like I'm..." Control complexity level
"Give me X options..." Get multiple alternatives to choose from
"What questions should I ask about..." Generate critical thinking prompts
"What am I missing..." Identify blind spots in your thinking
"Steelman the opposite view..." Get the strongest counterargument

Ethics Quick Reference

‚úì Generally OK

  • Getting feedback on YOUR writing
  • Creating study materials
  • Practice questions and quizzes
  • Grammar and spelling checks
  • Outlining and organization help
