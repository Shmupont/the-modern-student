Pricing FAQ

The $20 Course gives you lifetime access to all lessons, templates, and workflows. The $5.99/month Membership includes everything in the Course PLUS community access, live coaching calls, new monthly content, and direct support. Members get the full experience; course buyers get the core toolkit.
If you cancel your membership, you lose access to the community and coaching calls. If you previously purchased the $20 course, you keep course access forever. If you only had the membership, you lose access to everything when you cancel—but you can always buy the $20 course to keep the core content.
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on the course. For memberships, you can cancel anytime with no penalty. If you're not satisfied for any reason, email us and we'll make it right.
Most tools we recommend have free tiers that work great for students. We'll show you which free options are best and when paid versions might be worth it. The course itself is your only required investment.
