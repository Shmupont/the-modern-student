The Feynman Technique Helper

Use this when you need to test your understanding of any concept. Paste your explanation and AI will find the gaps.

Inputs Required
  • ‚Ä¢ The concept you're trying to understand
  • ‚Ä¢ Your explanation in your own words
The Template 
I want to test my understanding of [CONCEPT].

I'll explain it to you as if you're a complete beginner with no background knowledge.

After I explain, please:
1. Point out any gaps or inaccuracies in my explanation
2. Ask me 2-3 follow-up questions to test deeper understanding
3. Suggest analogies I could use to explain it better

Here's my explanation:
[YOUR EXPLANATION]
Example Input

Concept: Photosynthesis

My explanation: "Plants take in sunlight and turn it into food. They use their leaves to catch the sun and then make glucose which gives them energy to grow."

AI Response (Summary)

Gaps identified:

  • Missing: CO2 and water as inputs
  • Missing: Oxygen as an output
  • Missing: Chlorophyll's role

Follow-up questions:

  1. Where does the plant get the carbon atoms for glucose?
  2. Why are leaves green?
  3. What happens to photosynthesis at night?
